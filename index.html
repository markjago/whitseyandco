About the builder

Someone who shows up, does it properly, and answers the phone.

I started Whitsey & Co. in 2016 after a career teaching in secondary schools. What I wanted was simple: to work directly with the people I'm building for, understand exactly what they need, and finish every job feeling proud of the result.

Most of my work is kitchens and bathrooms — the rooms that matter most in a home. I only take on jobs I know I can do properly, and I work solo on most of them, so you always know who's turning up and who's responsible. Based in St Ives, Cambridgeshire. Around 50 projects completed and counting.
