Wait on the hero — watch it breathe

Named after the documentary filmmaker, the Ken Burns effect gives a static photo a sense of motion. The image gently zooms and pans over ~18 seconds, then reverses — so it never feels repetitive.

Combined with the text fade-in on load, it creates a polished, premium first impression before the visitor has scrolled at all.
