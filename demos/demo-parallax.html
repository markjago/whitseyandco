About

Scroll back up to see the effect

As you scrolled down, you'll have noticed the hero image appeared to drift upward more slowly than the page content — that's the parallax effect. It's achieved with a single CSS property: background-attachment: fixed.

It's one of the most widely used scroll effects on the web — understated, smooth, and works well with strong photography.
